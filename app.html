Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is EngTools Pro and who is it for? ▾
EngTools Pro is an AI workspace designed specifically for engineers. It helps electrical, mechanical, civil, petroleum, aerospace, and software engineers generate professional reports, analyze datasheets, compare components, and access engineering standards — all in minutes rather than hours. It is built for individual engineers, graduates, consultants, and engineering firms.
How is this different from ChatGPT or generic AI? ▾
Generic AI like ChatGPT gives you a blank prompt. EngTools Pro gives you pre-built engineering templates, auto-referenced standards (IEC, BS, ASTM, NFPA, API, ISO, and more), structured report sections, one-click refinement, visual datasheet parameter extraction, and formatted, submit-ready output. Engineers are not paying for AI words — they are paying for faster, structured, standards-aware engineering documentation.
Is the AI-generated output accurate enough to submit professionally? ▾
EngTools Pro produces high-quality, professionally structured engineering documentation from your notes and data. However, all AI-generated content should be reviewed by a qualified engineer before submission. The tool is designed to eliminate 80–90% of documentation time — you provide the engineering judgment and review the output. Always verify against applicable standards and site conditions.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time? ▾
Yes. All paid plans can be upgraded, downgraded, or cancelled at any time. There are no lock-in contracts. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period. Annual plans save 20% compared to monthly billing.
What happens to my generated reports? ▾
Reports are saved locally in your browser (localStorage) and can be exported as HTML or copied as plain text. Cloud storage and team sharing features are available on Team and Enterprise plans. We do not share, sell, or use your engineering data for training purposes.
Does the Enterprise plan support private deployment? ▾
Yes. Enterprise plans include options for private cloud deployment, SSO integration, custom company standards libraries, and dedicated onboarding support. Contact our sales team at hello@engtoolspro.com to discuss your organisation's requirements.
How do I get started? ▾
Sign up for a free account (no credit card required), select a report template, add your project notes and observations, and click Generate. Your first professional engineering report will be ready in under 2 minutes. The free plan includes 10 AI queries per day and 3 templates — enough to evaluate the full workflow.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

EngTools Pro collects the email address and name you provide during registration. We do not collect, store, or process the engineering content, project notes, or reports you generate — this data remains in your browser's local storage only.

How we use your data

Your email is used for account authentication and, where you have consented, for product updates. We do not sell your data. We do not use your engineering content to train AI models.

AI processing

When you generate a report, your notes and observations are sent to the Anthropic Claude API for processing. This data is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. We recommend not including personally identifiable client information in your report notes.

Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save your session, projects, and activity. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
