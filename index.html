FAQ

Common questions.

Why is this better than just using ChatGPT or Claude?▼
Generic AI tools give you a blank chat box. You need to know how to prompt them correctly, and they don't know what sections a Commissioning Report needs vs a Failure Analysis Report. They also frequently hallucinate standard numbers. EngTools Pro has 10 domain-specific templates, 60+ real engineering standards pre-loaded, and structured workflow — you just fill in your data and get a professional report. No prompting expertise needed.
Is the output actually good enough to submit professionally?▼
Yes — that's the entire point. Reports include correct section structures for the chosen report type, properly cited engineering standards, numbered and prioritised recommendations, data presented in tables, and safety-critical findings flagged clearly. Hundreds of engineers have submitted EngTools Pro output directly to clients and regulatory bodies with only minor personalisation.
What engineering fields does it cover?▼
Electrical / Electronics, Mechanical, Civil / Structural, Software / Systems, Chemical / Process, Petroleum / Oil & Gas, Aerospace, and Telecommunications. Each field has its own set of relevant standards pre-loaded. If your field isn't listed, you can add custom standards manually.
Can I upload PDF datasheets?▼
PDF upload with full text extraction is available on the Professional plan and above. On the free plan, you can paste the datasheet content as text — which works well for most spec sheets.
How does the 7-day free trial work?▼
When you upgrade to any paid plan, you get 7 days completely free with full access. If you cancel within those 7 days, you're charged nothing. After 7 days, your subscription begins. No surprises.
Is my data secure?▼
All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store your report content or datasheet uploads on our servers — they're processed in memory and discarded. Payments are processed by Stripe and we never see your card details. Enterprise plans can request a data processing agreement.
